How It Works

From sessions to auto-managed skills

AutoSkills learns your communication style, your intent patterns, and the way you frame problems — allowing the model to understand your requests faster and more deeply with every session.

1

Collect sessions

The CLI captures your AI coding sessions in the background — prompts, responses, tool calls.

2

Analyze patterns

System identifies recurring friction points — scope creep, missing constraints, repeated mistakes.

3

Build skills

Personalized skill rules are compiled into a managed bundle — categorized, versioned, explainable.

4

Shadow evaluate

New skills are verified against recent sessions before deployment. Low-confidence rules are held back.

5

Auto-deploy

Proven skills deploy to your agent automatically. You see what changed, why, and the measured impact.

The cycle repeats automatically. Every session makes your agent smarter — no manual tuning required.
